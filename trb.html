TRB allows users get daily physical therapy at home.
The Statistic and Plan funtion can help the
users to organize and track their excersice data.

Light

It is important for patients to excersice regularly, not only during the treatment
but also after the treatment, which can ensure you to restore mobility and
regain strength, as well as help to prevent any future injuries.
TRB is light and easy to carry along, patients can use it whereever they were.

Easy to set on

The benefits of physical therapy for seniors are increasingly well-known.
And due to the rapidly aging population, the need for their services is
expected to skyrocket in the years ahead.
TRB is easy to set up and use, therefore TRB is a friendly product to
all ages.

Combine with APP

Combine with APP can provides more funtion and more friendly to use.
For example, plan the excersice schedueland checkout the excersice statistic.

The tension spring in the resistance band to calculate the length when
pull the resistance band.

By adjusting the screw to fix the device at the edge of table, the
screw can be fixed to any kind of table.

Use handheld heart rate monitor to monitor the heart rate during
excersice.

Daily exercise is the key to excellent health.
