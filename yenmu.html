Design Concept

A chair combine both interaction and lathe technology.
From our sample 100, a shape suitable for a seat cushion is combined into a seat surface, combined with a spring.
As the seat cushion changes up and down, the seat surface change too, the lathe cushion will decrease with the weight of the user, which can bring more interaction with the user. The blue dye on the upper half of the wood is like a wave of problems in life.
As long as you relax, you can feel floating, and all the problems can be solved.

Wood Experiment

Test the color absorption of various wood species and the performance of various protective paints, as well as some special surface treatments, such as burning, planing, etc.
In order to find the most suitable wood species and protective paint.
After testing the color absorption of various wood species, beech was selected as the wood species most suitable for combining blue dyes, we also made a blue dye color sample with beech.

Color Chart of Indigo Dyeing

We use beech to made a color chart of Indigo dyeing, which can let us be easily applied to the work, showing the desired depth.
We got two dyeing cylinders, one with light color, another is the darker color one.
The first row soaked in a light color cylinder for one minute;
the second row is soaked in a light color cylinder for 2 minutes;
the third row is soaked in a darker cylinder for 1 minute;
the fourth row is soaked in a darker cylinder for 2 minutes;
the fifth row is soaked in a darker cylinder for 3 minutes.

Modeling Sample

Examined the shapes of up to 100 different lathes, tried different shapes, different materials, and different blue dyeing techniques.
Select the shape that can achieve the best shape and color absorption at the same time and apply it to the work.

After a series of experiments,
our team, six of designers designed a series of interesting and practical household items
with the theme of wood lathe combined with natural indigo dyeing.
In order to make the craft closer to our daily life instead of an ornamental art.
